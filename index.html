Ruyi Bang 如意金箍棒

The mythical Ruyi Jingu Bang is an iconic symbol of power in Chinese mythology. It's a giant magical weapon wielded by the legendary Monkey King, Sun Wukong.

About

Legend has it that Ruyi Jingu Bang is quite literally "whatever its wielder wishes it to be” – and this incredible weapon is not just one of immense size and strength but also possesses magical abilities that give Sun Wukong control over nature itself.

Its power could turn mountains into plains, part rivers into two, cut through metal like butter, and even counteract any enchantment used against its master.

The immortal saga King Gesar even cites this weapon as the source of divine inspiration for warriors on the path towards enlightenment.
